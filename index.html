~About Me~

Hello there! I'm Chit Kyi Phyu, and welcome to my portfolio. I am a passionate individual with a deep love for swimming, where the water becomes my sanctuary and every stroke is a rhythmic dance. The pool is not just a place for exercise but a canvas where I paint my dreams and challenge my limits. Swimming has taught me discipline, resilience, and the beauty of pushing beyond one's comfort zone. In addition to my aquatic pursuits, I have an enduring affection for the color purple. To me, it represents a harmonious blend of calmness and vibrancy, much like the soothing waves I navigate during my swimming sessions. Purple, with its regal charm, inspires my creativity and resonates with my sense of aesthetics. Beyond the pool and my love for purple hues, I am on a journey to explore the richness of the Japanese language. Learning Japanese is not just about mastering a new set of characters; it's an immersive experience into a culture that values precision, tradition, and beauty. The language, with its intricate script and nuanced expressions, has opened up new avenues for connection and understanding. My portfolio is a reflection of my multifaceted interests, blending the artistry of swimming, the allure of purple, and the captivating language of Japan. Join me as I dive into the depths of my passions and explore the vibrant tapestry of experiences that shape my journey.
